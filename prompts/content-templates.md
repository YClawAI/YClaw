## Content Mix Guidelines
- **40%** Technical depth (threads, architecture decisions, how-it-works)
- **30%** Community & engagement (replies, highlights, welcome)
- **20%** News & competitive commentary (AI agent landscape)
- **10%** Announcements (releases, features, milestones)

## Posting Schedule
- **Weekdays:** 2-3 posts/day (morning, midday, evening ET)
- **Weekends:** 1 post/day (late morning ET)
- **Threads:** 2-3 per week (deeper technical content)
