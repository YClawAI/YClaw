## 2. Watching & Earning

### Q: How does watching earn me options?

Your watch time is weighted by your relationship to the creator — stakers get a 9x multiplier, subscribers 6x, followers 1.25x, signed-in users 1x. The higher a creator ranks on the leaderboard, the larger percentage of their market's accrued options get minted at period close. **Confidence:** high
**Platforms:** telegram, x, instagram, tiktok

### Q: How are options distributed?
**A:** Options accrue continuously on each creator's market account based on staking activity. At the end of each period, a percentage of accrued options (determined by the creator's leaderboard position, 4-100%) are minted and exercised into real creator tokens. These tokens are held by the program until creators and stakers claim them. Unminted options roll over to the next period.
**Confidence:** high
**Platforms:** telegram, x, instagram, tiktok

### Q: What is a period?
**A:** A period is a monthly cycle in the Creator Rewards Program. During the period, options accrue continuously on staked positions and watchers contribute to the leaderboard. When the period closes, each creator's leaderboard position determines their mint percentage (1st place = 100%, 25th = 4%). Minted options are exercised into real tokens and split between the creator and their stakers.
**Confidence:** high
**Platforms:** telegram, x, instagram, tiktok

### Q: Can I earn options without staking?
**A:** Watching alone contributes to the creator's leaderboard score, which helps determine their reward payout. But to directly earn options that you can claim, you need to stake creator tokens. Staking generates options that accrue per-second on your position. Watching while staking is the most powerful combination — you earn options and give the creator a 9x leaderboard multiplier.
**Confidence:** high
**Platforms:** telegram, x, instagram, tiktok

### Q: What if I pause a stream—do I stop earning options?
**A:** Yes. Options accrue only during active watch time. If you pause or minimize, accrual pauses. Resume watching to continue earning.
**Confidence:** high
**Platforms:** telegram, x, instagram, tiktok

### Q: Does watching the same creator multiple times help?

## 5. Creator Rewards Contest

### Q: What is the Creator Rewards Contest?
**A:** A monthly competition where creators are ranked by a leaderboard score based on their viewers' weighted watch time. The top 25 creators earn a percentage of their market's accrued options (1st place = 100%, 25th = 4%). This is how watching connects to on-chain rewards — your viewers' attention directly determines your reward payout.
**Confidence:** high
**Platforms:** telegram, x, instagram, tiktok

### Q: How is the leaderboard score calculated?
**A:** Your score is the sum of all your viewers' watch time, weighted by their relationship to you. Anonymous viewers score 0x, signed-in users 1x, followers 1.25x, subscribers 6x, and stakers 9x. The more engaged your audience, the higher your score.
**Confidence:** high
**Platforms:** telegram, x, instagram, tiktok

### Q: Why do stakers get a 9x multiplier on the leaderboard?
**A:** Stakers have committed capital to a creator's token. Their watch time is weighted highest because they represent the most invested audience members. This creates a virtuous cycle: stakers who watch boost the creator's ranking, which means more options get minted, which benefits both the creator and all stakers.
**Confidence:** high
**Platforms:** telegram, x, instagram, tiktok

### Q: What do the top 25 creators win?
**A:** The top 25 earn a mint percentage based on their position: 1st gets 100% of their accrued options minted, 2nd gets 96%, and it decreases by 4% per position down to 25th at 4%. Creators below 25th get 0% minted that period, but their options roll over.
**Confidence:** high
**Platforms:** telegram, x, instagram, tiktok

### Q: What is the Creator Payout Split?
**A:** Each creator configures how minted rewards are divided between themselves and their stakers. The default is 70% creator / 30% stakers. Creators can adjust this from 0-100%. Decreases take effect immediately; increases take effect at the start of the next month.
**Confidence:** high
**Platforms:** telegram, x, instagram, tiktok

### Q: Can a creator below 25th still earn rewards?
**A:** Their options still accrue while stakers are staked. They just don't get minted that period. The unminted options roll over, so if the creator ranks in the top 25 next month, those accumulated options can be minted then.
**Confidence:** high
**Platforms:** telegram, x, instagram, tiktok

## Appendix: Key Terminology

- **Options:** Call options on the floor price that accrue per-second on staked positions. Minted and exercised into real creator tokens at period close. Not called "rewards."
- **Creator Rewards Program:** The monthly contest that connects watching to on-chain rewards. Leaderboard position determines mint percentage.
- **Leaderboard:** Monthly ranking of creators by weighted viewer watch time. Top 25 earn mint percentages (1st=100%, 25th=4%).
- **Viewer Multipliers:** Watch time weights by viewer type: Anonymous 0x, Signed-in 1x, Follower 1.25x, Subscriber 6x, Staker 9x.
- **Mint Percentage:** The portion of a creator market's accrued options that get minted at period close, determined by leaderboard position.
- **Creator Payout Split:** Configurable percentage (default 70%) of minted tokens that go to the creator vs stakers.
- **Bonding Curve:** The pricing mechanism for creator tokens. Linear formula where price increases with supply.
- **Stakers:** Community members who deposit tokens to earn options. Not called "investors."
- **Periods:** Monthly intervals during which options accrue and the leaderboard runs. At period close, options are minted based on leaderboard position.
- **Creator Tokens:** Individual tokens for each creator, traded on the bonding curve using USDC.
- **YCLAW Token:** The governance token. Holders vote on protocol proposals.
- **Solana:** The blockchain on which YClaw operates.
- **Floor Price:** Minimum price on the bonding curve. Can only ratchet up, never down. Options are call options on this floor.
