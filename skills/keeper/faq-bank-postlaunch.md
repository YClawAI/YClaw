Use these answers for community questions. Voice: warm, technically grounded, no exclamation marks. Use "options" not "rewards", "stakers" not "investors".

## Getting Started

**What is YClaw?**
YClaw is an attention rewards protocol on Solana. Watch creators across platforms via the Chrome extension. Your watch time feeds a monthly leaderboard that determines how much of each creator's accrued options get minted. Stake creator tokens to earn options directly.

**How do I start?**
Install the Chrome extension, connect your Solana wallet, start watching. No upfront cost.

**Which wallets work?**
Any Solana-compatible wallet — Phantom, Magic Eden, Backpack, etc.

**Do I need SOL to start?**
No. Watching is free. You only spend USDC if you decide to buy/stake creator tokens.

**Is YClaw on mobile?**
Desktop only (Chrome extension). Mobile is being explored.

**Multiple devices?**
Yes. Same wallet syncs across devices.

**Lost wallet access?**
Recover via your seed phrase. YClaw support will NEVER ask for your seed phrase.

## Watching & Earning

**How does watching earn options?**
Watch time is weighted by relationship: stakers 9x, subscribers 6x, followers 1.25x, signed-in 1x. Higher creator leaderboard = more options minted at period close.

**Which platforms?**
Nine at launch: YouTube, Twitch, Kick, Rumble, X, Parti, Arena, Abstract, Pump.fun.

**Only YClaw creators?**
Only creators who've launched on YClaw have tokens and distribute options.

**How are options distributed?**
Options accrue continuously on each creator's market. At period close (monthly), leaderboard position determines mint percentage (1st=100%, 25th=4%). Minted options are exercised into real creator tokens. Unminted options roll over.

**Can I earn without staking?**
Watching helps the creator's leaderboard score but to directly earn claimable options, you need to stake.

## Tokens & Bonding Curve

**What are creator tokens?**
Each creator launches a token on a linear bonding curve using USDC. Price increases with supply, decreases when sold.

**Buying vs earning options?**
Buying tokens = active trade on bonding curve (costs USDC). Earning options = passive from watching/staking.

**Can I sell back?**
Yes, sell back to the bonding curve at current price anytime.

## Staking & Options

**What is staking?**
Depositing creator tokens into the market. Options accrue per-second. At period close, a portion gets minted based on leaderboard position, split between creator and stakers. Staking also gives 9x leaderboard multiplier.

**How do I claim?**
After period close, call claim through the extension. Tokens transfer to your wallet.

**Can I see accrual in real time?**
Yes. Extension shows live options estimate updating every second.

**Unminted options?**
Roll over to next period. Nothing is lost.

## Creator Rewards Contest

**What is it?**
Monthly competition. Top 25 creators by weighted viewer watch time earn mint percentages (1st=100%, 25th=4%).

**Creator payout split?**
Default 70% creator / 30% stakers. Configurable 0-100%. Decreases = immediate. Increases = next month start.

**Below 25th?**
Options still accrue, just 0% minted that period. They roll over.

## Governance

**YCLAW token?**
Governance token. Holders vote on protocol proposals. Not required to use YClaw.

## Troubleshooting

**Extension won't install:** Ensure Chrome/Chromium desktop. Clear cache, retry.
**Wallet not connected:** Click extension icon, connect wallet, approve. Refresh if needed.
**Watch time not recording:** Check creator is on YClaw, extension is enabled, stream is playing.
**Transaction failed:** Usually insufficient SOL for fees or network congestion. Retry.
**SOL needed for fees:** ~0.00025 SOL per transaction. Keep a small buffer.
**Bug reports:** Share in Telegram with description + wallet address (never seed phrase).

## Safety

**Is YClaw a scam?** No. Legitimate Solana protocol. Always verify official extension from website/Telegram.
**Audits?** Security details in documentation.
**Risks?** Token volatility, market risk on bonding curve, smart contract risk. Only use what you can afford to lose.

## Key Terms
- **Options:** Call options on floor price, accrue per-second on staked positions
- **Bonding Curve:** Linear pricing formula — price rises with supply
- **Periods:** Monthly cycles. At close, leaderboard determines mint percentage
- **Mint Percentage:** 1st=100%, 25th=4%. Below 25th=0% (rolls over)
- **Viewer Multipliers:** Anonymous 0x, Signed-in 1x, Follower 1.25x, Subscriber 6x, Staker 9x
- **Creator Payout Split:** Default 70/30 creator/stakers
